SNESMonitorRatio

 Monitors progress of the SNES solvers by printing the ratio of residual norm at each iteration to the previous.

Synopsis

#include "petsc-private/snesimpl.h"   
PetscErrorCode  SNESMonitorRatio(SNES snes,PetscInt its,PetscReal fgnorm,void *dummy)
Collective on SNES

Input Parameters

snes - the SNES context
its - iteration number
fgnorm - 2-norm of residual (or gradient)
dummy - context of monitor

Keywords

SNES, nonlinear, monitor, norm

See Also

SNESMonitorSet(), SNESMonitorSolution()
