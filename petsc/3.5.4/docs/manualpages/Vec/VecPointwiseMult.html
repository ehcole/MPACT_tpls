VecPointwiseMult

 Computes the componentwise multiplication w = x*y.

Synopsis

#include "petscvec.h"   
PetscErrorCode  VecPointwiseMult(Vec w, Vec x,Vec y)
Logically Collective on Vec

Input Parameters

x, y -the vectors

Output Parameter

w -the result

Notes: any subset of the x, y, and w may be the same vector.

See Also

VecPointwiseDivide(), VecPointwiseMax(), VecPointwiseMin(), VecPointwiseMaxAbs(), VecMaxPointwiseDivide()
